Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Macrotarian ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and name (if provided). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information from these services.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including restaurants viewed, meals logged, and feature interactions. This helps us improve the app experience.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to show nearby restaurants. You can disable this in your device settings at any time.

Photos: If you use our meal scanning feature, photos are processed to identify food items. Photos are not stored permanently on our servers and are deleted after processing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To show you nearby restaurants with nutrition information
  • To track your meal history and macro goals
  • To process payments for premium subscriptions
  • To send important updates about the service
  • To improve and personalize your experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate the app (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your data is stored securely on servers provided by Supabase, which maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, go to Profile > Privacy Settings in the app, or contact us at privacy@macrotarian.com.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
